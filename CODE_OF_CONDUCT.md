# Code of Conduct

This organization is dedicated to a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

We expect all participants to communicate thoughtfully, assume good intent, and treat others with respect and kindness across all repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community spaces.

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and other abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated.

Organization maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that do not align with this standard.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the organization maintainers or via [GitHub's reporting tools](https://docs.github.com/en/communities/maintaining-your-safety-on-github/reporting-abuse-or-spam).
